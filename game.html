Tank Game:

The tank game is a 2D shooter game that features attacking and defending against other tanks. It is made from scratch in javascript (Including the game engine, animation and score system). This is version 1.2. GAME UNDER DEVELOPMENT.

PLAY PC

PLAY MOBLIE

How to play (pc):

  1. Use W, A, S and D to move in the game space. Your mouse guides the direction of the tank. Click your mouse to shoot. You gain a point, everytime you destroy a tank. Try and beat your highscore.
  2. You have some health. If those flamethrowing tanks touch you, you lose life very quickly. So avoid them.
  3. The missiles are non guided (unlike in version 1.1)
